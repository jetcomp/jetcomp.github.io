  • Virus and malware removal 

  • Virus and malware removal

    Sometimes a device is beyond cluttered or infected, and needs serious intensive care. For such occasions, we take a targeted, surgical approach to eradicate any traces of viruses, trojans, ransomware, and every nasty sort of bug in between.

    If you have ever attempted to simply delete a virus, you have probably run into situations where the virus returns all by itself, the system starts experiencing errors, or both. Authors of malicious software use every trick in the book to keep you captive, and that’s where Jet Computers comes in.

    By placing your system in total isolation, it becomes possible to fight back against malware or other unwanted software without giving it a chance to defend itself. After an infection has been cleared up, we continue to closely monitor system activity until we are convinced that the culprit is not lying in wait to once again rear its ugly head next time you sit down to check your email.

    If this sounds a little overkill, our basic cleaning (detailed above) may be more your speed. In fact, many scary-looking infections are not so tough after all, and we will never charge you for additional procedures that you don't need.
